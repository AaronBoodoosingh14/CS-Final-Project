Instructions

  1. Step 1: Upload a carrier image - Click on the "Home" link in the navigation menu.
  2. Step 2: Fill in the message - In the form, choose a carrier image file by clicking on the "Choose File" button. Select a JPEG or PNG image file from your device.
  3. Step 3: Encrypt and Save - Enter your secret message in the "Message" field. Click on the "Encrypt and Save" button to encode the message within the carrier image.
  4. Step 4: Download the steganographic image - Go to the "Download" page by clicking on the "Download Menu" link in the navigation menu. Click on the "Download" button to retrieve the steganographic image.
  5. Step 5: Decrypt the message - Use a steganography decryptor tool to extract the hidden message from the downloaded steganographic image.
